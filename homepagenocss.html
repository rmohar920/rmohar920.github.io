About Monk's Cafe

Monk's cafe was established in 1989 by Larry the cook who is the owner/manager/cook. Larry had dreams of starting a resturant that was welcoming for friends to hangout grab a coffee or enjoy a reasonably priced meal. Here and Monk's Cafe you might run into some of your favorite regulars such as Jerry, Kramer, George and Elaine. While you're here please try our world famous hands on appitzers and for those who are on a diet try our big salad
